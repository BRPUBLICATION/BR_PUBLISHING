"Entrepreneurship" From Ideas to Impact

One of the key objectives of this book is to bridge theory and practice. Real-world case studies, illustrative examples, and actionable frameworks are incorporated throughout to help readers understand how entrepreneurial concepts are applied in diverse contexts. By integrating lessons from successful startups, global innovation hubs, and social enterprises, the book provides both inspiration and practical guidance for readers aiming to launch, manage, or invest in entrepreneurial ventures.

Another central focus is the role of personal development and mindset in entrepreneurship. Attributes such as resilience, creativity, adaptability, and leadership are essential for navigating uncertainty and overcoming challenges. This book emphasizes not only the technical aspects of business creation but also the personal growth and self-awareness required to thrive as an entrepreneur.

The intended audience for this book includes students, aspiring entrepreneurs, business professionals, and educators. It serves as a resource for understanding the entrepreneurial ecosystem, developing strategic and operational skills, and gaining insights into emerging opportunities in technology, social impact, and global markets. By combining foundational knowledge with contemporary trends, the book prepares readers to make a meaningful impact in the world of entrepreneurship.

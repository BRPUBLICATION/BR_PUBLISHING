MuleSoft in Action: Autonomous APIs and Microservices with AI

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, organizations are increasingly adopting API-led integration and microservices architecture to drive innovation, agility, and scalability. MuleSoft, a leader in integration and API management, provides a powerful platform for designing, building, and managing APIs and microservices.

This book explores the intersection of MuleSoft, APIs, microservices, and artificial intelligence (AI). As organizations strive to create more intelligent, autonomous, and adaptive systems, the integration of AI with MuleSoft's API-led approach offers unprecedented opportunities for innovation and growth.

Through practical examples, case studies, and expert insights, this book serves as a comprehensive guide to designing, building, and managing autonomous APIs and microservices using MuleSoft and AI. You’ll explore topics such as API-led integration, microservices architecture, AI-powered integration, and creating self-healing APIs.

Whether you're a developer, architect, or technical leader, this book equips you with the skills to build intelligent, scalable systems using MuleSoft and AI. Discover how to design APIs with MuleSoft’s Anypoint Platform, integrate machine learning models, and enable adaptive systems for future-ready enterprises.

INTELLIGENT CLOUD MIGRATION: Unifying Squads, KPIs, and AI to Transform Business Agility

In today's fast-paced digital landscape, cloud migration has become a critical imperative for businesses seeking to enhance agility, scalability, and innovation. However, the journey to the cloud is often complex, requiring careful planning, strategic alignment, and technical expertise.

This book explores the intersection of cloud migration, team collaboration, performance metrics, and artificial intelligence (AI). By integrating these elements, organizations can unlock the full potential of cloud computing, drive business agility, and achieve sustainable success.

Through a combination of practical insights, real-world case studies, and expert analysis, this book provides a comprehensive guide to intelligent cloud migration. It examines how to build high-performing teams, define and track KPIs, leverage AI to optimize cloud transitions, and transform operations to adapt to evolving markets.

Whether you're a business leader, IT professional, or technology enthusiast, this book equips you with the strategic and technical knowledge needed to migrate successfully to the cloud and unlock innovation, agility, and long-term business value.

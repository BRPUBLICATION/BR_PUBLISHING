Artificial intelligence and the art of communication: Insights from ChatGPT

This book, Artificial Intelligence and the Art of Communication: Insights from ChatGPT, seeks to explore this transformation from multiple perspectives. It examines the intersections of AI, linguistics, ethics, and social interaction, offering a holistic understanding of how AI-mediated communication is influencing our personal, professional, and educational lives. By delving into real-world applications, research findings, and theoretical insights, this work provides readers with a comprehensive view of both the possibilities and the challenges presented by AI communication technologies.

One of the central aims of this book is to demystify AI as a communicative agent. While ChatGPT and similar systems are often perceived as "intelligent," their intelligence is fundamentally different from human cognition. Through careful analysis, we distinguish between simulated empathy and genuine understanding, automated reasoning and human judgment, and algorithmic assistance versus relational interaction. Readers will gain not only technical insights but also critical frameworks for evaluating the ethical, psychological, and societal implications of AI-mediated dialogue.
